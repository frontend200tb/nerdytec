Nerdytec Couchmaster Cycon.
Sofa table for computer.

Play your favorite games while sitting on the couch.

This patented system uses only the finest materials such as leather and microfiber, allowing you to control your computer from your sofa in the most comfortable environment.

Couchmaster is a great platform for long hours of gaming or ergonomic work using your TV or projector.

Up to 4 devices can be connected to the built-in USB 3.0 connectors (for example, a mouse, keyboard, headphones, etc.). The system works without additional power supplies.

The Couchmaster connects to your computer via a 5m USB 3.0 cable (can be extended up to 10m if needed).

Excellent ergonomics.

“From a health point of view, I can say that Couchmaster offers the best option for ergonomic work at a computer or laptop.

Large pillows allow you to relieve stress on the muscles of the shoulder and neck, which helps prevent muscle spasms."

- Rolland Kellerbach, physiotherapist, Cologne - Delbruck, Germany.

Cable management.

All excess wires are easily removed inside the countertop. Any peripheral devices can be connected as you like.

Couchmaster is equally comfortable for both right-handers and left-handers.

You can also attach the keyboard to the tabletop using the included Velcro tape.
